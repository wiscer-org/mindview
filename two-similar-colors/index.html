Two Similar Colors

Welcome! Your goal is to see the two similar color side by side in the screen.

With screen reader being activated, you can swipe right or left to move focus, and double click to activate the focused button.

To know the 2 colors, swipe right/left until 'Show answer' button is focused. After clicked, a dialog will pop-up with information of the current colors and their relative position. Swipe again to find a button to continue to next round, to reshuffle similar colors and the position.
