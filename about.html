What is Mind View?

Mind View is an interactive collection of tools designed to help you practice and improve your ability to see while blindfolded. Each tool is optimized for use on tablets and iPads with a screen reader enabled. Learn more about this further down the page.

What is "seeing blindfolded"?

Seeing blindfolded, also known as vibravision, mindsight, or other similar names, refers to the ability to perceive one's surroundings visually without using the eyes. You can find numerous videos about this on YouTube by searching for terms like "mindsight", "vibravision", or "seeing blindfolded". For those who speak Indonesian, there are many demonstration videos available from a martial art called Merpati Putih that show this unique ability in action.

What is a screen reader?

A screen reader is an application typically used by individuals who are visually impaired. It reads aloud what is displayed on the screen, enabling users to navigate and interact with computers, tablets, or phones without needing to see the screen. Android-based devices come with a built-in screen reader called TalkBack, while iOS devices include VoiceOver.

Using a screen reader is straightforward: swipe left or right to move focus to the previous or next element. To activate an element, such as a button, simply double-tap when it is in focus.
