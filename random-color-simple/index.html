Welcome to Random Color Simple! Your goal is to see the color of this page.

With screen reader is activated, you can swipe right or left to move focus, and double click to activate the focused button.

To know the current color, swipe right/left until 'Show answer' button is focused. After clicked, a dialog will pop-up with information of the current color. Swipe again to find a button to continue to next round, to reshuffle color.
